Abstract

Taking into account the development of the Virtual Reality in the last years, this work presents the main forms of VR, considering its participation in the creation of immersive experiences, that is the main purpose of this work. In this way, it is necessary to present respectively, among other, the notions of Cyberspace, Cybertime, Space and Absolute Time that allow a general vision of the area. Following, the several immersive forms of VR, we uttered, also, the forms of Telepresence, Hypertext and Cyborgs, proposing them as a new interface paradigm with the user. Through examples, the existent applications of VR are focused in several areas of the knowledge. Therefore, the present work counts with a vast bibliographical resource obtained through several research forms (Internet, Books, Centers of Research and Development, Published Works and Lectures), which, in a certain way, makes to stand out its importance as source for consultation.
